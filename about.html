About Us

About GoblinPass

At GoblinPass, we believe password security should not be complicated.

Why GoblinPass was created

Many people reuse the same password across websites, often making tiny changes such as adding a symbol or changing a number at the end. When one website is breached, attackers may try similar versions elsewhere.

A simple alternative

GoblinPass was created to make it easier to use strong, unique passwords without needing to remember hundreds of different passwords or rely entirely on a normal online vault.

Security first

Your online security is only as strong as your weakest link. Protect your email account, use unique passwords, enable two-factor authentication where possible, and avoid simple password variations.

Our mission

To help people build better password habits, improve their online security, and take control of their digital lives without sacrificing convenience.
