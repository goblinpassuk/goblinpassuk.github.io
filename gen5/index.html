How secure storage works

Your master password is encrypted with AES-GCM. The encryption key is derived from a WebAuthn PRF secret that is released only after device verification. Only encrypted data, random salts, and the passkey identifier are stored in this browser.

Windows Hello may use your face, fingerprint, or device PIN. Clearing site data or deleting the passkey removes access to the saved copy, so keep the original master password somewhere safe.
