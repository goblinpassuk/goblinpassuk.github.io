Local-first password security with YubiKey PRF

Generate Strong Passwords Without Storing Them

GoblinPass is a local-first/stateless password generator with optional YubiKey password-ingredient support.

Create unique passwords from inputs you control, keep generation on your device, and add a YubiKey PRF factor for high-value accounts when supported by your browser, operating system, and hardware key.

YubiKey PRF becomes part of the password recipe

GoblinPass does not use YubiKey as normal two-factor login. When YubiKey mode is enabled, the generated password depends on PRF data returned by the physical key.
